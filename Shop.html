Online Student Ordering Options

1. The first option is a WISH KIT that is all-inclusive (with the exception of the Friday double-header basketball game). In each Wish Kit is a BAGP shirt and wristband. With the wristband, students get into all of the events throughout the week (Vista Idol, Ruff n' Tuff, Wrestling, Hockey, and Community Fair) for FREE. This is to encourage involvement and participation. Students are encouraged to purchase wristbands as it is the easiest and cheapest way to participate in all Wish Week festivities! Wish Kits are $30 and will get students into every event for the week, except the basketball game on Friday. If you do not have a wristband (adults/spectators included) there is a $5 entry fee to each event.

2. The second option is to purchase the VIP WISH KIT. This kit will also get students into events for free, with the exception of the basketball game on Friday. The VIP Wish Kit includes the Wish Week shirt, wristband, BAGP custom sticker, custom make a wish socks, Wish Sticker, Make-A-Wish keychain, and a raffle entry to meet the wish kid. VIP kits are $50 and ONLY AVAILABLE ONLINE FROM NOW THROUGH JANUARY 20TH!!! You can purchase it in person starting January 1st.

3. Mountain Vista students cannot pre-order just the Wish Week T-shirt. They will have to wait to purchase just the $25 t-shirt when we sell shirts starting Jan 31st in the L500s.
